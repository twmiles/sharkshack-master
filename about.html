About Us


Shark Shack was created by three partners named Tracy Miles, Rayder Kopetsky, and Noah Tucker in 2010. The idea came
from their mutual love for seafood and the state of California. Shark Shack has won restaurant of the year 4 times and was
the runner up in 2017.We are located at 401 Santa Monica Pier in Santa Monica, CA and plan on opening up two more
locations in the future!
